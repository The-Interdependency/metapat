# Test Spec: Theorem 05 — Consciousness Is Optional

## Claim

Consciousness is one observer-mode.

A non-conscious simplex may perform registration.

Story is conscious registration of time.

## Procedure

1. Define sequential tensor alteration.
2. Define non-conscious registration, such as a log, layer, trace, or state change.
3. Confirm observer role exists without consciousness.
4. Define conscious registration as story.
5. Confirm story is a specialized observer-mode, not the parent of time or registration.

## Passing condition

The model accepts non-conscious registration as observer role.

The model accepts story as conscious registration.

The model does not require consciousness for time, registration, observer role, or object-instantiation.

## Failing condition

The model makes consciousness necessary for time or registration.

The model makes story identical to time.

## Minimal assertion

```text
registration can be non-conscious
story = conscious registration of time
```
